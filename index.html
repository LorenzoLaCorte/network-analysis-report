Network Analysis

Abstract

The following documents showcase our assignments for the course of Network Analysis.

The first assignment consists in the analysis of all the main metrics of the chosen networks.

The other two consist in conducting and reporting the results of simulations on networks:

  • In the second document we report the results of simulating an epidemy in our networks, to recognise what are the key parameters affecting the spreading.
  • The third focuses on simulating different types of attacks to analyze networks robustness (resiliency to failures).
